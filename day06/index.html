Advent of Code

Day 06

Instructions

Evaluate a set of instructions to turn on elements in a 2 dimensional array. The instructions can be the following:

ACTION x1,y1 through x2,y2

With action being:

  • turn on
  • turn off
  • toggle

Part 1. How many elements are turned on?

Part 2. What the total brightness if on increased brightness by 1, off decreases brightness by 1 and toggle increases brightness by 2?
